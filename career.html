Requirements and salary

The average salary of an engineer in the U.S. is $100,640 per year and it can be anywhere from $64k per year to $170k per year depending on what kind of job you have. The requirements to become an engineer are having some type of engineering degree that can vary depending on what type of job you are looking for along with some past work experiance.
